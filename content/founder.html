The founder story

From the executive office to systems innovation.

A decade close to C-suite decision-making revealed a recurring pattern: enterprise friction often begins where leadership clarity, human behaviour and operating systems stop working together.

Jesunifemi's legal training sharpened her approach to governance, structure and intellectual property. Her work as an Executive Assistant to C-suite leaders gave her direct exposure to enterprise operations and a $50M+ executive portfolio. Her systems practice includes Harvard Business School's Design Thinking and Innovation program, alongside training in applied psychology, life coaching and emotional wellness.

Blue Corridor Global brings these disciplines together through executive advisory, corporate development, legacy codification and institutional human capital systems.
